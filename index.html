About TINKERFY

TINKERFY is a technology and innovation brand focused on robotics, electronics, programming, 3D design and hands-on engineering projects for learners and makers.

What we do

We create exciting tech content and projects that combine hardware, software, design, problem solving and creativity. From robots to prototypes, TINKERFY is all about learning by building.
